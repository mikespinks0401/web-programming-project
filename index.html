Meeting snapshot

How meetings work

We start with a prompt, define terms, and argue positions with reasons. No one is forced to talk. Listening still counts as learning.

  • Open to students who are curious, new, or already deep into philosophy
  • Short readings and concrete prompts keep things moving
  • Respectful disagreement is expected, not avoided
