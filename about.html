About the club

A place to test ideas, not perform certainty.

The San Jac Philosophy Club is built around discussion, reading, and live reasoning. Students don’t need prior philosophy coursework to join. Curiosity is enough.

Get involved Ask a question
A

What the club is for

Big questions, sharper reasoning, and a welcoming place for students who want more than surface-level conversation.

B

How the room works

We define terms, challenge assumptions, and make room for disagreement without turning discussion into noise.

C

Who should come

Students who like ethics, politics, consciousness, technology, logic, or simply asking better questions.

What students can expect

Simple structure. Real conversation.

  • Discussion nights built around a prompt or big question
  • Reading circles using short, approachable texts
  • Guest perspectives from faculty or students
  • Open participation. Quiet students are welcome too.
